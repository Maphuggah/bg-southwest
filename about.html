Precision Operations for Surface Care

Commercial floor care shouldn't be unpredictable. When we analyzed the San Diego maintenance industry, we saw a critical lack of operational rigidity—teams showing up late, inconsistent chemical applications, and poor communication.

So, we applied an operational mindset to facility maintenance.

Today, B & G Southwest delivers exact, uncompromising standards. When our specialized floor technicians arrive at a Class A office park in Sorrento Valley, they deploy with route-verified timing, precise chemical dosing protocols (utilizing advanced Diversey and Ecolab tech), and zero margin for error.
